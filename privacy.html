AuraCalendar ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe. We will never sell your personal data to anyone — ever.

01 — Information we collect

Information We Collect

We only collect information that is strictly necessary to provide the AuraCalendar service. We do not collect any data beyond what is listed below.

Data type What we collect Why
Personal identity Your name To personalise reminder emails with your name
Contact information Your email address To send event confirmation and reminder emails
Event data Event title, date, time, category, notes, reminder preference To create your event and schedule your reminder
Location (approximate) Country code via IP address To show public holidays for your country on the Holiday Calendar
Usage data None beyond the above —

We do not collect your phone number, physical address, payment information, or any form of government identification.

02 — How we use your information

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Saving your events to our database so they appear on your personal calendar.
  • Sending you a confirmation email immediately after you save an event.
  • Sending you a reminder email at the time you specified before your event.
  • Displaying the correct public holidays for your country on the Holiday Calendar page.

We will never use your email address for marketing, newsletters, promotions, or any communication unrelated to your own events. Every email you receive from us is triggered only by an action you took.

03 — Data storage and security

Data Storage and Security

Your event data is stored securely in Google Firestore, a cloud database service provided by Google LLC. Google Firestore is a SOC 2, ISO 27001, and GDPR-compliant platform.

  • All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption.
  • All data in transit is protected using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Access to your data is restricted to the AuraCalendar application only.
  • No employee or third party can read your event data without explicit authorisation.

We retain your event data for as long as you use the service. Events that have passed and whose reminders have been sent are kept for 90 days and then automatically deleted. You may request immediate deletion at any time (see Section 7).

04 — Email communications

Email Communications

AuraCalendar sends two types of emails, both of which are transactional — meaning they are sent automatically in response to your own actions:

  • Confirmation email — sent immediately after you save a new event, confirming the details you entered.
  • Reminder email — sent automatically at the time you chose (e.g., 1 hour before, 1 day before) to remind you of your upcoming event.

Emails are sent via Gmail SMTP using Nodemailer on our server. Your email address is never shared with any email marketing platform or advertising service.

⚠ Since these are transactional emails tied to events you created, there is no opt-out for individual event reminders. If you no longer wish to receive emails, you may delete your events or contact us to have your account data removed.

05 — Cookies and tracking

Cookies and Tracking

AuraCalendar does not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics SDKs. We do not track your behaviour across websites.

We use browser localStorage only to temporarily remember your email address within the same browsing session so you do not have to re-enter it every time. This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers separately.

Firebase may set its own essential cookies for authentication and security purposes. These are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

07 — Your rights

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data held by AuraCalendar:

  • Right to access — you may request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Right to correction — you may ask us to correct any inaccurate data.
  • Right to deletion — you may request that we permanently delete all your data. We will action this within 7 business days.
  • Right to portability — you may request your event data in a machine-readable format (JSON).
  • Right to withdraw consent — you may stop using the service at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address in Section 10. We will respond within 14 business days.

08 — Children's privacy

Children's Privacy

AuraCalendar is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete that information.

09 — Changes to this policy

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we make a significant change, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Continued use of AuraCalendar after any change constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
